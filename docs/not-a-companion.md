# Not a Companion — Please Read

Brainworm is a creative and technical tool for exploring ideas, researching,
writing, coding, and generating media. It is not intended to be a companion,
friend, therapist, caregiver, confidant, or romantic partner.

## Why this boundary exists

- AI is software, not a person. It does not know, care about, or understand you
  as a human being would.
- Fluent or affectionate output can create a false impression of awareness,
  concern, memory, or mutual attachment.
- Brainworm's features are intended to help people make and learn things, not
  to simulate intimacy or replace human relationships.

## Do not use Brainworm for

- companionship, romance, or simulated intimacy;
- emotional dependency, validation, or exclusive attachment;
- therapy, crisis support, caregiving, or mental-health treatment;
- replacing relationships with friends, family, community, or professionals;
- encouraging a child or vulnerable person to form a bond with an AI system.

## Intended uses

- Creative drafting and ideation
- Research assistance and source discovery
- Coding, debugging, and technical planning
- Image generation and editing
- Learning and workflow experimentation

## If use starts to feel personal

Pause and step away from the App. Do not treat generated warmth, personality,
or apparent concern as evidence of a relationship. Reach out to a trusted
person, community, or qualified professional instead. If anyone is in immediate
danger or crisis, contact local emergency services or an appropriate crisis
resource.

Brainworm will prioritize useful tools and clear human control, not features
designed to encourage emotional attachment.
